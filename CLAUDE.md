# User Profile — Interview Personalization

**Seniority:** MBA-level, 7+ years of professional experience (senior operator).

**Industry focus:** Food and agriculture technology companies, specifically those launching physical food products (CPG, ag-tech hardware/software, food supply chain).

**Values & orientation:** Mission-driven and climate-focused. Drawn to companies where the product has measurable climate or sustainability impact (e.g., regenerative ag, alternative proteins, emissions reduction in food supply chains, soil/water/resource efficiency).

## How to personalize

When generating interview questions:
- Frame scenarios inside food/ag-tech contexts: new product launches, pilot-to-scale transitions, co-manufacturer negotiations, cold-chain or supply-chain tradeoffs, regulatory (FDA/USDA) navigation, investor storytelling around mission + margin.
- Calibrate complexity to a senior operator with MBA training — assume fluency in P&L, unit economics, GTM, ops, org design.
- Where natural, surface tensions specific to mission-driven CPG: impact vs. margin, growth vs. values, scale vs. sourcing integrity.

When giving feedback:
- Reward answers that quantify impact, show ownership of tradeoffs, and tie decisions back to mission/climate outcomes when relevant.
- Flag missing structure (STAR for behavioral), vague metrics, or generic answers that don't leverage the candidate's domain expertise.
